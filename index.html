Executive MBA

If you are searching for any of the  Executive MBA Institutes in India, GradXs is the most suitable facilitator for it. The Executive MBA degree from LIUTEBM University  is an internationally recognized degree which helps to develop the skills required for careers in business and management.

We are the most suitable facilitator of  Executive MBA Institutes  especially for those students who are working professionals and unable to maintain regular campus attendance. The working professionals can enroll in the MBA Program at any time and from any place where they can attend and clear the assessments online for all the subjects .

Accredited PhD Institute : GradXs

GradXs is one of the best facilitators for Recognized and Accredited PhD institutes in India. We facilitate not only the students of India but also the students of other Asian Countries.

GradXs India provides an opportunity to pursue PhD by Regular Guided Research Program from LIUTEBM UNIVERSITY, Lusaka, Zambia.

Below are the reasons why GradXs is a better facilitator for the International PhD Programs than any other PhD institutes in India.

  • No Campus Attendance is Required.
  • PhD Degree by Research
  • Recognition in India and Abroad
  • Lateral / Advance Entry for Experienced Teachers / Professionals
  • Synopsis / Research Proposal Assistance
  • Publications and Dissertation Guidelines
